    What is AI Readiness?

    Measuring what matters most right now

    AI Readiness measures how seriously a college or university is preparing its students for a world where artificial intelligence is reshaping every industry and profession. Our proprietary research and algorithms have identified five key pillars that determine how well a school has adapted to the AI revolution, and by extension, how prepared its graduates will be for what comes next.

    Why it Matters

    The stakes have never been higher

    The gap between AI-ready graduates and everyone else is widening fast. Students who graduate from institutions that have embedded AI into their curriculum, culture, and strategy will have a measurable advantage in hiring, in earnings, and in career trajectory. Where you go to school has always mattered. Now, how AI-ready that school is matters just as much.
